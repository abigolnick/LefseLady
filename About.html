About Me

My name is Abigail (though I prefer Abby) Golnick. I am a senior at Western Illinois University in Macomb, Illinois; I am majoring in Creative Writing and minoring in American Sign Language. I plan on attending university a little bit longer to obtain my Master of Fine Arts degree in Creative Writing in the hope that I can teach at the college level.

I like to spend my time outside of academics writing, reading, and crocheting. I am the proud mom to a one year old bearded dragon named Lizzie. I am one hundred and ten books away from having a library in my apartment in Kewanee, Illinois.

About This Website

This website was coded by me in Github using the Verti template from HTML5up website for my Writing for the Web (Eng 388). All semester we have been exploring foodways, creating Instagram posts, and writing blog posts to lead up to this website.
